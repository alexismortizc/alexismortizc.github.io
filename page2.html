Sustainability

The demand for products and services from companies that are economically, environmentally and socially sustainable is increasing. We give you the tools so that you can demonstrate to your clients the sustainability of your company.

  • ENVIRONMENTAL EVALUATION OF THE SITE - Evaluation of agricultural and forestry lands based on the Environmental Site Assessment protocol: Phase I Environmental Site Assessment Process for Forestland and Rural Property (ASTM E2247-16). This evaluation addresses environmental and social aspects of an agricultural and forestry plantation.
  • SUSTAINABILITY CODE WINE INDUSTRY - Audit, training and advice on the implementation of the sustainability codes of the Chilean wine industry.
  • HAZARDOUS WASTE HANDLING - Evaluation of the level of compliance with regulations for the management of hazardous waste and development of waste management plans.
  • REGISTRATION OF EMISSIONS AND TRANSFER OF POLLUTANTS (PRTR). Support in mandatory declarations of emissions and waste in Single Window (PRTR).
  • ENVIRONMENTAL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM ISO 14001 - Diagnostic audit and advice on the implementation of the ISO 14001 Environmental Management System.
  • ANALYSIS OF THE LIFE CYCLE OF A PRODUCT. Inputs and outputs are calculated throughout the life cycle of a product and the positive and negative impacts on the environment are calculated.
  • SUSTAINABILITY REPORTS - Description and calculation of sustainability indicators and preparation of sustainability reports according to the Global Reporting Initiative (GRI)
  • SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY - Audits and implementation of Social Responsibility Standards (SMETA, Walmart's Ethical Standard, Costco's Code of Ethics, etc.)
  • SUSTAINABILITY MANAGEMENT FOOD & AGRI - Diagnosis of business sustainability, Development of strategies, design of sustainability management systems, Project Finance in non-conventional renewable energies and energy efficiency and biomass in agribusiness and waste management.

Agricultural Business Management

We provide support to our clients who seek improvements in the way of planning and implementing their business operation, to achieve competitiveness in their processes through.

  • STUDIES OF COSTS, EFFICIENCY AND PRODUCTIVITY - Optimize the resources used by companies that allow, in economic terms, that the project generates greater profitability.
  • BUSINESS PLAN - The Business Plan in the company is the guide that takes your business idea to reality and to today's competitive environment. During its elaboration, errors and doubts can be identified before the start-up, and to plan in advance the variables that will lead your project successfully. As part of the development of a business plan, the following services are carried out. a) Analysis of the business model, b) Marketing plan, c) Operations and HR plan, d) Financial plan.
  • PROJECT ASSESSMENT - Companies that seek advice in the analysis of investment alternatives or in search of business niches, the financial evaluation service is provided, guaranteeing at the same time the expected economic profitability of the invested capital. Among the services we provide this a) Evaluation of purchase versus lease, b) Evaluation of suppliers and new business models, c) Risk management for silvoagricultural environmental projects with Montecarlo simulation.
  • ECONOMIC VALUATION OF AGRICULTURAL COMPANIES - For acquisition of agricultural assets, sale or income of investors with discounted cash flow methodology from the investor's point of view. Extensive technical, economic and process engineering knowledge of the forestry and agricultural sector.
  • AGRICULTURAL APPRAISALS -  Appraisals are the answer to the need to value the goods depending on the use, location and purpose of the appraisal
  • FINANCIAL MODELING - Projection of Financial Statements, Simulation of various economic scenarios, Budget of investments and expenses, Evaluation of maximum capacity of indebtedness versus capacity of payment of the company or project.
  • INTERMEDIATION AND FINANCIAL PLANNING - Financing and restructuring of debt with banks, preparation and presentation of financial statements to banks and creditors, advice on financial derivatives, cash flow management, financing with leasing, factoring or confirming, construction of guarantees.
  • MANAGEMENT CONTROL - Measurement and monitoring of the main key performance indicators (KPI) that affect the performance of the different business processes through the implementation of scorecards and scorecards.

Quality and Food Safety

In the food industry quality assurance and safety are the most important aspects that must be addressed by companies. Our technical team can support you in the following topics.

  • SYSTEMS OF QUALITY MANAGEMENT AND FOOD SAFETY - Audit, training and advice on ISO 9001, Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Point System - HACCP (based on Codex Alimentario), Good Manufacturing Practices (based on the Food Codex), World Standard of Food Safety of the British Retail Consortium (BRC), International Food Standard (IFS), Food Sanitary Regulation.
  • FOOD DEFENSE - Food defense is not the same as food safety. The defense focuses on the protection of the food supply against deliberate contamination by chemical substances, biological agents or other harmful substances applied by people who wish to harm us. Our company will help you develop and implement a food defense plan that allows you to comply with the recommendations established by the US Food Drug Administration.
  • TRACEABILITY - Support in the development and implementation of an effective traceability system for your products or raw materials.
  • REMOVAL SYSTEM (WITHDRAWAL) AND RECALL (RECALL) OF A PRODUCT - Our company will support you in the development of an effective system of withdrawal and recovery of the product and in emergency situations where you must face the decision to recover or withdraw your product from the market .

About us

Greennova is a company founded in 2003 with the purpose of delivering solutions on issues of sustainable intensification, quality management systems and food safety and agribusiness to companies in the forestry and agribusiness sector of Latin America.
For this, we have a team of multidisciplinary professionals, trained in recognized universities, both in our country and abroad.
In addition to the above, Greennova is part of a network of collaborative work with university entities and professionals specializing in specific areas that require a high level of specialization, which allows you to take advantage of synergies that make it possible to offer comprehensive solutions and address high-level issues. complexity and / or specialization.
